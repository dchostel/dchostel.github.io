Directions

Woodley Park Metro

 Turn left and then right outside the escalators. Walk to the first set of lights (the corner of Calvert and Connecticut). Turn left and keep walking over the bridge. Calvert runs into 18th Street. Walk down 18th Street about 100 meters and we are about a restaurant called "The Diner".

Union Station

 From Union Station, take the #96 bush, which stops in front of the train station, and get off at 18th Street. Alternatively, you could also that the Red Line metro to Woodley Park, and then follow the directions above to walk to the hostel from the metro station.

National Airport

 From Reagan National Airport take the Metro Yellow line at the Reagan Metro stop and take it to the U Street NW Metro stop. Once you exit the Metro at U Street NW you must cross over U Street NW and catch any #90, #94, #96, #98 bus, which will take you to 18th Street NW in Adams Morgan.

Dulles Airport

 Take bus 5A to the corner of 7th and D streets. The bus comes every 40 min and takes one hour from the airport. From there, transfer to the Metro and take the Yellow or Green Line to Columbia Heights. Walk south on 14th Street and then turn right on Columbia. Walk about 5 blocks until you get to the corner of 18th Street. Turn left on 18th and we are about 50 yards down.

BWI Airport

 Take the B30 bus from the airport to the Greenbelt station on the Green Line. From there, take the metro to the Columbia Heights station. Then walk south on 14th Street to Columbia Road. Turn right on Columbia and walk 5 blocks to 18th Street. Turn left on 18th and we are about 100 meters down the street.
